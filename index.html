This’ll be, interesting. Hope you like it. I see myself as a gamer, a person, and a developer. In that order. I primarily like to play online games, but will play single player games when in the mood. I don’t really fit into a gaming profile. So lets move on to developer. I am by no means certified in any way, however that doesn’t mean I do not enjoy this, at times. I have yet to use Go, C#, Rust, or really Python for that matter, however they all have my interest for their own reasons. I do most of my development on Github, and primarily use either TypeScript or JavaScript with NodeJS. Node can be infuriating at times and is not a be all and all by any means. One last bit before I end this, I can enjoy Linux but most of the time its just infuriating.
